## 🚨 Troubleshooting

**If messages don't send:**
1. **Click the gear icon** ⚙️
2. **Click "Test Connection"**
3. **Check your n8n workflow** is running
4. **Verify the webhook URL** is correct

**If styling looks wrong:**
- **Refresh the page** (F5)
- **Try a different browser**
- **Clear browser cache**
